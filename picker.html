In principle, this picker is perfectly accurate, provided you pick consistently - you'll correctly get your second favorite second,
even if it's pitted against your absolute favorite early on, for instance. You can reorder your favorites after you've found them, but in principle you shouldn't need to.
DISCLAIMER: You're free to pass as many times as you like, but in order to find your favorites
and narrow down your pool of pets, it's suggested that you pass as little as possible!
